Our Journey

From Our Home to Yours ‚ú®

Every great recipe starts with a memory. For us, it starts in our mother's kitchen.

As the years passed and we grew up‚Äîmoving out, getting married, and traveling‚Äîone thing always brought us back: Ramadan. It was the time for family gatherings, and more importantly, it was the time for Mom's legendary Kunafa and Golash.

She didn't just make sweets; she made masterpieces, perfected with her own special "extras" that no store could ever replicate. We realized her talent was too good to keep hidden within our four walls. We wanted the world to taste the love, the crunch, and the tradition that we grew up with.

We named the brand after the newest light in our family, Layla (Loly), bridging the gap between our mother's timeless tradition and a sweet future for the next generation.
